Our Story

Founded on the principles of structural integrity and strategic foresight, Raha Group began as a specialized consultancy with a singular focus: redefining how human ambition interacts with physical and digital infrastructure.

Over three decades, we have evolved into a diversified global powerhouse. From the heavy industrial backbone of Raha Infra to the transformative educational horizons of Learnixa, our trajectory has been defined by a commitment to "Architectural Prestige."
